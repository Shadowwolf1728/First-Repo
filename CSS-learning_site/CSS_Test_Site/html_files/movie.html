Summary

Within this movie, We follow Miles Morales, a normal high school student who lives in New York CIty. This normalcy is broken when Miles is bitten by a radioactive spider in an abandoned subway station he was in while painting graffiti. With this, he gains power equal to spiderman, tries them out, and then returns to the station to find the actual Spiderman going against the Kingpin who is dabbling in parallel universes for one reason or another. He and Spiderman try to go against him but sadly Spiderman dies by being pushed into the collider and gives Morales the way to disable it through a USB. After this Morales eventually finds an alternate reality of spiderman(Known as Peter B. Parker) and meets Gwen Stacy who is another spiderman. With them(and other spidermen they meet along the way) they are able to deactivate the collider with Peter B. Parker sacrificing himself to do it.

Summary

Within this movie we follow Marty Mcfly from the quiet town of Hill Valley California is his sad family of a depressed mother and meek bullied father. On a night unlike most others, Marty meets his scientist friend Emmet “Doc '' Brown who shows that he has built a time machine from a delorean powered by plutonium stolen by terrorists. As they are marveling at it, previously talked about terrorists gun them down and they escape into the time machine and go back 30 years in the past. They are unable to go back to the future because they lack plutonium so Marty decides to explore his past town. During this, he changes the past through taking the place his father should have been in some events and having his mother become infatuated in him. Luckily he is able to course correct and return back to the future(the movie title!!) where he finds his father confident and mother happy because of his exploits.

Link for Movie
  • Steven Spielberg
  • Sam Neill

Summary

Within the movie Jurassic Park, we begin on the island of Isla Nublar where the mind of Dr.Alan Grant and the money of rich industrialist John Hammond help create a theme park using cloned dinosaurs. While on this island we follow the grandchildren of Hammond, Lex and Tim who were invited to the island and join a tour group to see all of the dinosaurs there. Their time on the island is cut short as a tropical storm approaches and they return to their electric tour vehicle to return to safety. As they are doing this, a very discontented computer programmer bribed by a corporate rival of Hammond shuts down the tour vehicles and the park’s electric fences. This is done as the tour group is near the Tyrannosaurus enclosure and are attacked by it. They are luckily able to escape it with a large amount of trouble and leave the island through a helicopter.
